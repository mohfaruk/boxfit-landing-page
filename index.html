Box&Burn | Boxing Classes

At Box&Burn our mission is to provide provide the best boxing classes on offer. We eat, sleep and breathe boxing and we want to share our passion for boxing with the public. For this reason we have tailored our classes so everyone can have the time of their lives getting into fighting shape!

We offer an extensive range of programmes providing a mixture of solid self-defence coaching, and strength & conditioning to ensure your time in our gym is always enjoyable. If that isn't enough, we also provide personal coaching and nutritional guidance so you have access to the necessary resources to become the best version of yourself. It is for these reasons Box&Burn has been innovating the boxfit space throughout the United Kingdom. Their award-winning programmes led by some of the best boxing coaches in the country have transformed the fitness industry, proving you can enjoy a social workout session whilst working towards your goals.
